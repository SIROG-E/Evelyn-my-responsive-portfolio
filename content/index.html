About Me

My name is Evelyn Goris. I am originally from Peru, South America. I moved to the U.S. to pursue a medical degree. Due to complicated situations, I had to put my dreams of becoming a Neurologist on hold. It is during the 2020 pandemic that I decided to enroll in the Full Time Coding BootCamp at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I hold an Associates Degree in Science. Graduated with magna cum laude and was in the President's List during the entire I was enrolled at Lone Star College. I am fluent in Spanish, English and some Portuguese.

My family is everything to me. I have two children, Sophie and Gabriel. I have two younger siblings, Jorge and Karla, and a niece, Chaia. My parents live abroad, but visit four times a year. I love spending time with my entire family. Some of my hobbies include kayaking, party planning, sewing, reading, among other things.
